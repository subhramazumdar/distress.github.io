DISTRESS 2026

Decentralized Immutable Secure Tamper-Resistant Encrypted Searchable Storage
5 June 2026 | Brisbane, Australia
Workshop at IEEE International Conference on Blockchain and Cryptocurrency (ICBC) 2026

About

The DISTRESS workshop aims to advance the intersection of blockchain technology and searchable encryption by fostering research on practical systems that integrate blockchain's immutability, auditability, and incentive mechanisms with cryptographic techniques for privacy-preserving search over encrypted data.

Objectives

  • Bridge theory and practice in searchable symmetric encryption (SSE) and blockchain deployments
  • Explore blockchain-specific constraints and solutions for SSE
  • Evaluate system trade-offs in blockchain-SSE architectures
  • Foster cross-disciplinary collaboration
  • Catalyze real-world applications in regulated data marketplaces and decentralized healthcare

Topics of Interest

Search & Encryption

  • Searchable symmetric encryption schemes
  • Verifiable SSE and succinct proofs
  • Privacy-preserving index commitments
  • Merkle and accumulator commitments

Blockchain Integration

  • On-chain key management
  • State-channels and rollups
  • Off-chain storage architectures
  • Token economics for search services

Applications

  • Private LLM retrieval
  • Decentralized healthcare records
  • Supply-chain archives
  • Regulated data marketplaces
